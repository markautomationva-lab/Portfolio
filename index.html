GHL CRM Management

GoHighLevel is one of the most powerful all-in-one platforms for growing businesses — but only if it's set up and running the right way. I build and manage your entire GHL ecosystem: pipelines, automations, funnels, workflows, and sub-accounts — configured to convert leads, retain clients, and scale your operations without adding headcount.

  • Sales pipeline & opportunity management

  • Workflow automations & trigger-based actions
  • Sub-account setup & white-label configuration

Voice AI

Your phones are answered. Your leads are called. Your appointments are booked. All without a single human picking up. I build AI voice agents — powered by platforms like Vapi and Retell — connected to real phone numbers that hold natural conversations, take real action, and push data directly into your CRM and automation workflows the moment a call ends.

  • Inbound call handling — 24/7, never misses a call
  • Speed-to-lead outbound — calls new leads within 60 seconds of form submission
  • Appointment booking, rescheduling & cancellation handling
  • Automated follow-up & re-engagement call sequences
  • Live CRM updates & workflow triggers post-call

About Me

Who I Am

I'm Mark Anthony Andrada, an AI Automation Specialist based in Bracknell, England.

I help businesses eliminate inefficiencies, streamline operations, and scale without increasing administrative overhead. By designing intelligent systems that automate repetitive tasks, optimise customer journeys, and connect critical business processes, I enable teams to focus on growth instead of manual work.

My work spans workflow automation, CRM implementation, AI-powered communication systems, and operational process design. Whether it's automating lead management, improving customer follow-up, integrating business applications, or building conversational AI solutions that engage with customers in real time, I create systems that work reliably behind the scenes and deliver measurable results.

I take a practical approach to automation: understanding how a business operates, identifying bottlenecks, and designing solutions that remove friction across sales, marketing, customer service, and internal operations. The goal isn't simply to automate tasks — it's to build scalable systems that improve efficiency, reduce costs, and create better experiences for both businesses and their customers.

Since entering the AI automation space in late 2025, I've dedicated myself to continuously learning, building, and refining advanced automation solutions. Every system I create is designed with one purpose in mind: helping businesses reclaim valuable time, operate more effectively, and grow with confidence.
